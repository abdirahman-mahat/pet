Pretty Chihuahua

Interesting facts about them:

  1. Chihuahuas are the smallest dog breeds in the world. Between 2-6 pounds
  2. Excercise requirements less than 20minutes a day
  3. Longetivity range: 14-18yrs
  4. Are bred for companionship
  5. Energy level: Very high

Favourite Activities

    1.Find the Treat Game:All you need to do to start the game is show your dog a tiny treat or piece of kibble and you are set.

    Toss the food around the room.Continue increasing the distance by tossing it further. You will be able to feed your dog entirely by just playing this game.

    2.The Three Cup Game:All you need is three cups and a small ball.

    Place the three cups upside down on the floor, take the ball, show your pet, hide it under one cup.

    Using your hands, pass the cups around in order to mix the arrangement.

    Once you are sure the dog will not find it, you can request the pet to point the cup where the ball is. If they point correctly, you give them a treat.
